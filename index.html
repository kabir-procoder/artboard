Who are we?

Silent Reflection is a metal band based in Zürich Switzerland, founded by Salvadoran guitarist Juan Jeanpierre and Chilean drummer Valentino Anziani. The band was formed in 2021 with the intention of bringing a powerful metal sound and using it as a canvas to convey in words the need of facing inner fears and overcoming any obstacles in the busy life.

Their style fuses Orchestra sounds, powerful guitar riffs and choruses with meaningful lyrics centered on self-reflection to make you want to explore and embrace your darkest inner thoughts and use them to your advantage. They express their message through clean voices and growls.

In 2023, Silent Reflection's started working in their concept album "The Awakening", releasing on September 8th 2023 their first single called Breath and Reflect. Their second Single called “Shadow's Awakening” was released on November 20th 2023. In 2024, the band continues working in their next single called "Darkened Existence" and will be released on January 12th 2024. This will be the opening song of the concept album.
